About MMM Hall of Residence


Pt. Madan Mohan Malviya Hall of Residence was founded on the 27th February 2005 and completed in record time for occupation by the 2005-06 batch on July 22nd.
The bust of Pt. Madan Mohan Malaviya was unveiled by Justice Girdhar Malaviya on 30th March 2007.

It consist of four blocks : DSK Block, RR Block, SDS Block and HK Block.
